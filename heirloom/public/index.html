Questions, answered

What kinds of damage can it fix?

Fading and yellowing, scratches and dust, creases and small tears, water spots, and soft focus. It can also colorize black & white photos. Severely missing areas (a torn-away face) may not fully recover.

Do I need a scanner?

No. A phone photo of the print works well — lay the photo flat near a window, avoid glare, and fill the frame.

What happens to my photos?

They're processed, then automatically deleted from our servers after 60 minutes — purchased or not. They are never used to train AI models and never shared.

What if I don't like the result?

You see a preview before paying, and if a purchased photo still disappoints, reply to your receipt for a full refund — no questions asked.

What resolution do I get?

The full-resolution restored file, print-ready as JPEG. Frame it, gift it, or share it with the whole family.
